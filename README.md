# Habits Social: The User Guide

Habits Social is your companion for building better routines through social accountability. This guide covers how to master your habits, manage your history, and connect with your circle.

## 1. Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1.  Visit [www.habitssocial.com](https://www.habitssocial.com) and click **Sign Up**.
2.  Choose a unique username—this is how friends will find you.
3.  Once registered, you'll land on your personal **My Habits**.

### Personalizing Your Profile
*   **Access Settings:** Click your avatar at the top of the screen to open the Profile Modal.
*   **Identity:** Update your **Display Name** and choose an **Avatar** from our picker.
*   **Protection:** If you try to close the settings with unsaved changes, we'll alert you so you don't lose your work.

## 2. Mastering Your Streaks

My Habits is your mission control for daily habits.

### Adding a Habit
1.  Click **Add Habit**.
2.  **Name it:** Be specific (e.g., "Daily Journaling").
3.  **Frequency:** Set your goal (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly).
4.  **Sharing:** Toggle **Share** to let friends see your progress in their activity feed.

### Logging & History (The 14-Day Window)
You have total control over your recent history.
*   **Logging:** Click the status icon to cycle through **Complete** (increases streak), **Skip** (preserves streak), or **Fail** (resets streak).
*   **Retroactive Updates:** Missed a day? You can log or change your status for any habit up to **14 days in the past**. The app automatically recalculates your current and longest streaks across the entire timeline.
*   **Global Timezones:** We include a small buffer for future-dated logs to ensure users in all timezones can log accurately regardless of where they are in the world.

## 3. Connecting with Friends

Habit tracking is more effective when you aren't doing it alone.

### Building Your Circle
*   **Find Friends:** Use the search bar in the **Social** tab to find people by username.
*   **Favorites:** Star your closest friends to pin them to the top of your list for quick check-ins.
*   **Deep Dives:** Click any friend to see their shared habits, current/longest streaks, and a full monthly calendar of their activity.

### Privacy Management
You decide who sees what. Use the **Share Habits** tool in the Social tab to instantly update visibility settings for specific habits across your entire friend list.

## 4. The Activity Feed

The **Activity** tab is your live hub for social motivation.
*   **Smart Grouping:** When a friend shares several habits at once, the feed groups them into a single update to keep your timeline clean.
*   **Milestones:** Celebrate together as you or your friends hit major streak milestones (7, 30, 100 days).
*   **Journey Tracking:** Click any feed event to see the detailed history behind that specific habit.

## 5. Installing as an App

Habits Social works best as an installed app on your home screen.
*   **iOS:** Tap **Share** in Safari -> **Add to Home Screen**.
*   **Android:** Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome -> **Install app**.
*   **Desktop:** Click the **Install** icon in your browser's address bar.
