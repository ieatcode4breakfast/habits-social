# Habits Social: Your Guide to Better Habits

The live app is already available at [https://www.habitssocial.com/](https://www.habitssocial.com/).

Welcome to **Habits Social**! This platform is designed to help you build lasting habits by combining personal tracking with social accountability. Whether you're trying to drink more water, read daily, or hit the gym, Habits Social makes it easier to stay on track.

Here is a quick guide on how to get the most out of the app.

---

## 🧭 Navigation

Habits Social has four main sections, available from the top nav bar on desktop or the bottom tab bar on mobile:

- **My Habits** – Your personal habit dashboard.
- **Buckets** – Group related habits together.
- **Social** – Your activity feed and friends list.
- **Inbox** – Private chats with your friends.

---

## 🎯 1. Tracking Your Habits

The core of Habits Social is your daily habit list on the **My Habits** page.

**How to create a habit:**
1. Navigate to **My Habits**.
2. Tap the **Add** button (or the **+** icon on mobile).
3. Give your habit a clear, actionable name (e.g., "Read 10 pages"). You can also add a short **Description** for context.
4. **Skips:** Life happens! You can configure how many skips are allowed per **week** or per **month**. If you miss a day, using a skip won't break your streak as long as you have skips remaining for that period. You can also choose **No limit** for unlimited skips.
5. **Sharing:** If you already have friends on the platform, you can immediately choose which friends to share this habit with during creation.

> *Note: You can track a maximum of 30 habits at a time.*

**Logging your progress:**
Every day, tap a day cell on a habit's timeline row to open the **Log Menu**. The menu shows you the available actions based on your habit's skip settings:

- ✅ **Complete** – Mark the day as done.
- ➖ **Skip** – Use a skip (if you have remaining skips for the period).
- ❌ **Fail** – Mark the day as a failure (appears when no skips are left).
- 🌴 **Vacation** – Mark the day as vacation to protect your streak (appears when no skips are left). Vacation works just like a skip—there are no penalties or downsides to using it, and your streak stays fully intact. That said, we rely on your honesty here: please reserve Vacation for times you're genuinely away or unable to follow your routine, not as a loophole to avoid a fail.
- 🗑️ **Clear** – Undo a previous log entry.

You can update logs for the **last 14 days**, including today.

**Streaks:**
When you've completed a habit for 2 or more consecutive days, a streak badge appears next to the habit name. Streaks are color-coded by length:

- 🟢 **Green** (2–6 days) – You're building momentum.
- 🟣 **Purple** (7–29 days) – You're on a roll.
- 🟡 **Gold** (30+ days) – You've locked in a serious streak.

Streaks at 7+ days also show a 🔥 flame icon.

**Editing a habit:**
Tap on a habit's name to open its **Edit** view. From here you can:

- Rename the habit or update its description.
- Adjust skip settings.
- View a **monthly calendar** showing all your logged statuses.
- Choose which friends to share the habit with.
- **Delete** the habit permanently (with confirmation).

**Reordering:**
If you have more than one habit, use the **Reorder** button to drag and drop your habits into your preferred order.

**Pull to refresh:**
On mobile, pull down on the habit list to refresh your data.

## 🪣 2. Grouping with "Buckets"

Sometimes you want to track a broader goal that consists of several smaller habits. That's where **Buckets** come in.

**What is a Bucket?**
A Bucket is a collection of related habits. For example, you might have a "Morning Routine" bucket that contains the habits: "Make Bed," "Drink Water," and "Stretch."

**How to use a Bucket:**
1. Go to the **Buckets** tab.
2. Create a new Bucket, name it, and select which of your existing habits belong in it.
3. Each bucket shows a read-only timeline summary (using square cells) so you can see at a glance how the bucket is performing across the week.
4. **Expand** a bucket by tapping on it to reveal the individual habits inside. From the expanded view, you can log progress on each habit directly, or tap a habit to open its full edit view.
5. **Bucket Streaks:** As you complete all the habits within a bucket each day, the bucket itself maintains a "meta-streak," giving you a high-level view of how well you are sticking to that overall routine.
6. **Reordering:** Just like habits, you can reorder your buckets to keep your most important goals at the top.

## 🤝 3. Social & Activity Feed

Building habits is easier when you do it with friends! The **Social** tab is the social hub of Habits Social.

It has two sub-tabs: **Activity** and **Friends**.

### Activity Feed

The Activity feed shows a real-time, chronological log of things happening across your friend network—completions, streak milestones, vacations, and more. The feed groups events by date ("Today," "Yesterday," etc.) and loads more content automatically as you scroll down.

Each activity entry shows:
- The friend's **avatar** and **name** (tap to visit their profile).
- A **message** describing what happened (with color-coded streak information).
- A **visual habit grid** showing their weekly status at a glance.
- A **reply button** (💬) to quickly send a private chat message reacting to their activity.

You can also tap any activity entry to view the detailed habit history in a monthly calendar view.

### Friends Tab

**Adding friends:**
1. Switch to the **Friends** sub-tab.
2. Use the search bar to find people by their username.
3. Tap **Add** to send a friend request.
4. After sending or accepting a request, you'll be prompted to **share specific habits** with that friend.

**Managing friend requests:**
- Incoming requests appear in a collapsible **Friend Requests** section at the top (with a badge count).
- You can **accept** or **decline** each request.

**Your friends list:**
- Shows all your accepted friends and pending outgoing requests.
- Use the **filter bar** to quickly find a specific friend.
- Tap the ⭐ **star** icon to **Favorite** a friend. Favorites are pinned to the top of your friends list for quick access.
- Tap the ✕ button to unfriend someone or cancel a pending request (with confirmation).
- Tap a friend's name to visit their **profile page**.

### Friend Profiles

Each friend has a dedicated profile page showing:
- Their avatar, username, and how many habits they've shared with you.
- A **weekly timeline** and **monthly calendar** of their shared habit progress (read-only).
- Actions to **Share Habits**, **Favorite/Unfavorite**, **Accept/Add/Cancel Request**, or **Unfriend** depending on your relationship status.

**Sharing habits:**
- From a friend's profile, tap the **Share** button to choose exactly which of your habits to share with them. They won't see everything—only what you choose to make visible. You can update this at any time.
- You can also manage sharing on a per-habit basis from the habit editor (under "Share with").

## 💬 4. Inbox (Chat)

The **Inbox** is your private messaging system for chatting with friends.

**Starting a conversation:**
1. Tap the ✏️ **New Chat** button.
2. Select a friend from your friends list (with an optional filter).
3. Start typing!

**Chatting:**
- Messages appear in a familiar chat-bubble layout with timestamps.
- The input bar has a **character counter** (max 1,000 characters per message).
- **Reply to Activity:** When you tap the reply button (💬) on someone's activity in the Social feed, it opens the Inbox pre-loaded with a visual card of that activity as context for your message.
- **Message deletion:** You can delete your own messages within 5 minutes of sending them. Deleted messages show a "This message was deleted" tombstone. A confirmation modal appears before deletion.

**Conversation list:**
- Shows all your active conversations sorted by most recent activity.
- Each entry shows the friend's avatar, name, a preview of the last message, and the time of the last message.
- Unread conversations display a **badge** with the unread count.
- Pull down on mobile to refresh the conversation list.

**Deleting a chat:**
- Tap the 🗑️ trash icon in the chat header to delete your copy of the conversation. Your friend will still see their messages. This action cannot be undone.

## 👤 5. Personalizing Your Profile

Make the app your own by customizing how you appear to others.

1. Tap your username (e.g., "Hi, username!") in the top navigation bar to open **Edit Profile**. On mobile, tap your avatar on the My Habits page.
2. **Avatar:** Tap **Change Avatar** to open the avatar picker. Avatars are randomly generated in multiple styles—tap **Refresh** to get a new batch, or choose **Default** for a plain icon.
3. **Username:** Update your display name (3–20 characters).
4. **Email:** Update the email address associated with your account.
5. **Password:** Optionally change your password (minimum 8 characters). You'll need to confirm the new password.
6. All changes require a confirmation step before saving. If you try to close the profile editor with unsaved changes, you'll be asked whether to discard or keep editing.

*Note: Profile changes require an active internet connection. An offline warning banner will appear if you lose connectivity.*

## 🔐 6. Getting Started (Sign Up & Log In)

**Creating an account:**
1. On the login screen, switch to the **Sign Up** tab.
2. Choose an avatar (optional), pick a username, enter your email and password, then confirm your password.
3. Tap **Sign Up** to create your account.

**Logging in:**
- You can log in with either your **email** or your **username**, plus your password.

**Logging out:**
- Tap the logout icon (🚪) in the top-right corner of the navigation bar. Logging out securely clears all local data from the device.
