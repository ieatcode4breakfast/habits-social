## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
To start tracking, you'll need an account.

1. Head over to the app and click on **Sign Up** (or **Log In** if you already have an account).
2. Create a unique username and password. Your username is how friends will find you later.
3. Once logged in, you'll be taken straight to your personal Dashboard.

---

## 2. Mastering Your Streaks

Your Dashboard is your personal mission control. We've designed our streak system to be both motivating and forgiving.

### Adding a New Habit
1. Click the **Add Habit** button on your dashboard.
2. Give your habit a name (e.g., "Morning Workout", "Read 20 Pages").
3. Choose your **Frequency** (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly).
4. Choose whether to **Share** this habit. If enabled, your friends can see your progress and cheer you on.

### Understanding Your Streak
The visual indicators next to your habit tell you exactly where you stand:
* **Bright and Solid:** You've completed your habit for today. Your streak is active and growing.
* **Faded (Translucent):** You haven't logged yet today, but your streak is safe. It's waiting for your action.
* **Empty:** No active streak yet. Time to start.

### The Three Actions
Click the status icon to cycle through your daily report:
1.  **Complete:** You did it. Your streak increases by 1.
2.  **Skip:** Life happens. Skipping preserves your streak—it won't break, but it won't grow either. Perfect for rest days or travel.
3.  **Fail:** Resets your active streak to zero instantly. Use this for ultimate accountability.

### Tracking Progress
Keep an eye on the progress badges (e.g., "3/5 this week"). These help you stay on track for your long-term frequency goals, even if you don't log every single day. The streak pill itself always tracks consecutive days of action.

## 3. Connecting with Friends

Building habits is easier when you aren't doing it alone. Head over to the **Social** tab to build your circle.

### Adding Friends
Use the search bar in the Social tab to find friends by their username. Send them a friend request, and once they accept, you'll be able to see each other's public habits.

### Favorite Your Inner Circle
Have a few friends you want to keep a close eye on? Tap the star icon next to their name. Favorites are always pinned to the top of your list, ensuring you never miss their progress.

### Deep-Dive Profiles
Click on any friend to view their full profile. You can see:
* Every habit they've chosen to share with you.
* Their current and longest streaks.
* A detailed monthly calendar showing their exact logging history.

### Managing Privacy
You control who sees what. You can manage your shared habits directly from your profile, or use the share tool to quickly update your visibility settings for specific friends.

---

## 4. The Activity Feed

The Activity tab is your central hub for social motivation. It provides a real-time chronological timeline of your network's progress. 

* **Daily Logs:** See when friends complete, skip, or fail their shared habits.
* **Streak Milestones:** Celebrate together when you or a friend hits major streak milestones (like 7, 30, 100 days, or annual anniversaries).
* **Momentum Updates:** Watch as friends build and extend their streaks day by day. 

Clicking on any event in the feed brings up the detailed history of that specific habit, so you can see exactly how much work went into that milestone.

## 5. Install as an App (Mobile & Desktop)

For the best experience, install Habits Social directly to your device. It feels just like a native app, with faster loading and a dedicated icon on your home screen.

### On iOS (Safari):
1. Open the website in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with an arrow).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.

### On Android (Chrome):
1. Open the website in Chrome.
2. Tap the three-dot menu and select **Install app**.

### On Desktop (Chrome/Edge):
1. Click the install icon on the far right of your address bar.
