# Contact Support

Please email [support@habitssocial.com](mailto:support@habitssocial.com) for feedback, suggestions, and to report issues. 

When reporting issues, please provide a detailed description of the issue, including what is happening compared to what is supposed to happen, along with any error messages. 

**Example:**
* **What's happening:** After submitting a new habit, it doesn't appear on the list. 
* **What's supposed to happen:** The new habit should appear at the top of the habits list.

Screenshots and screen recordings showing the issue or error will be greatly appreciated!
